About GIIS Tech Club

GIIS Tech Club is the premier computing and innovation society run by students, for students. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world engineering, providing a collaborative sandbox for aspiring developers, designers, and innovators.

We organize hackathons, specialized bootcamps, and technical symposiums designed to elevate the skillsets of our members. From artificial intelligence architectures to scalable web platforms, we explore the tools shaping tomorrow's industry.

Joining our ranks means becoming part of a driven community of problem-solvers. Whether you are writing your first line of Python or optimizing distributed server deployments, the GIIS Tech Club provides the resources, mentorship, and network to scale your potential.

Our Legacy

2014

First Every GIIS Hackathon

Started a legacy event which would grow bigger over years, fostering a spirit of competition and innovation among students.

2024

Hackathon X

GIIS Hackathon goes free for the first time! This landmark event was fully funded by DBS, our generous sponsor, making technology accessible to all.

2024

Hack Club Affiliation

GIIS Tech Club officially affiliates with Hack Club and officially transitions into a Non Profit organization, joining a global community of markers.

2026

GIIS Tech Club 2K26

This is the most ambitious chapter yet, building next-gen projects, expanding partnerships, and pushing the boundaries of what student-led innovation can achieve at GIIS.
